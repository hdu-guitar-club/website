# Events

HDU Guitar Association hosts a rich variety of music events every year, providing members with ample stage performance opportunities.

## Annual Event List

| Event Name | Time/Frequency | Description |
|------------|----------------|-------------|
| Club Recruitment Fair | Every September | Main channel for new members to join |
| College Welcome Galas | Starting every September, 1-2 times per semester | Providing band performances for various college welcome events |
| Vine Corridor Concert | Held in first semester, 1-2 times per semester | Campus outdoor music activity |
| Lawn Concert | Held irregularly each semester | Relaxed and casual music gathering |
| New Year Music Festival/Small GR | Held at the end of December each year | Year-end music extravaganza |
| Club Festival | Held at the end of May each year | Campus club showcase activity |
| Top Ten Singers Final | Held at the end of May each year | Guitar Association responsible for live accompaniment |
| **GR Music Festival** | Held at the end of May or early June each year | **Annual core large-scale performance** |
| Cross-University Collaboration Stages | Held irregularly throughout the year | Cross-university exchange performances |

## GR Music Festival

GR Music Festival is the most important core large-scale performance event of HDU Guitar Association each year, held at the end of May or early June. This is an important stage for Guitar Association members to showcase their year-long practice results, and also an annual grand event for HDU campus music culture.

### Event Highlights

- Multiple bands performing on stage in rotation
- Various music styles competing on the same stage
- Professional stage equipment and lighting
- Participation of all teachers and students in the university

## Vine Corridor Concert

The Vine Corridor Concert is a signature outdoor music activity of the Guitar Association, held at the Vine Corridor in the living area of HDU Xiasha Campus. The following uses the March 29, 2024 session as a reference template to introduce the entire process:

### Event Process

#### 1. Early Preparation

- Publish warm-up posts on WeChat Official Account
- Set comment section as song request area, audience leaves messages for songs they want to hear
- Randomly select lucky audience members' song requests to add to the playlist
- Complete various early publicity work such as posts and poster production

#### 2. Mid-term Preparation

- Basically determine the final playlist based on song request results + band song selections
- Basically determine the performance order of each performance team

#### 3. Late Preparation

- Each performance team conducts rehearsals on their own
- Organize personnel to transport various instruments and equipment needed for the event to the Vine Corridor venue

#### 4. On-site Preparation

- Complete stage setup and equipment debugging
- Staff in place, complete various preparations before the performance
- Actors take the stage for rehearsal

#### 5. Official Performance

- Each team performs on stage in the established order
- Arrange personnel to take photos and record videos throughout the process

#### 6. Wrap-up Phase

- After the performance ends, transport all instruments and equipment back to the Student Activity Center South A105 rehearsal room
- Clean up the Vine Corridor site
- Operate various official accounts, edit and publish the photos and videos taken

## Cross-University Collaboration

The Guitar Association actively cooperates with various universities, providing members with cross-university stage performance opportunities, expanding their musical horizons, and meeting more music enthusiasts.
