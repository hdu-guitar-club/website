## Core Positioning

HDU Guitar Association is a live music club with **bands at its core**, based on the **four major band instruments (guitar, bass, keyboard, drums) + vocalist**.

## Club Features

- **Strong Inclusivity**: Welcomes various instruments and embraces all music genres
- **Live-Oriented**: Focuses on stage performance and live performance experience
- **Open Collaboration**: Cooperates with various universities, providing cross-campus performance opportunities
