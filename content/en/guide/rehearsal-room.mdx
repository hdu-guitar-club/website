# Rehearsal Room Guide

## Basic Information

| Item | Content |
|------|---------|
| Address | Student Activity Center South Building A105 |
| Opening Hours | 24 hours open (rehearsal room door is not locked, can enter at any time) |

## Area Division

The rehearsal room is divided into the following areas:

- Public Guitar Area
- Guitar Area
- Bass Area
- Keyboard and Synthesizer Area
- Drum Set Area
- Speaker Area

In addition, it is equipped with: cajon, uncommon instruments, various stands, microphones, stools, and other public items.

## Guidelines Access Channel

Detailed usage guidelines for the rehearsal room are available on the walls of the rehearsal room and in the announcements of the Guitar Association Newcomer QQ group.

## Instrument Storage

Members can place their private instruments in the rehearsal room without additional application.

## Core Usage Rules

### Public Item Usage

Members can freely use public items in the rehearsal room such as air conditioners, power strips, various stands, and stools.

### Pre-Departure Notes

1. **Turn Off Air Conditioner**: When leaving the rehearsal room, if there are no other members inside, remember to turn off the air conditioner
2. **Lock Door**: The student leaving last at night (usually around 10-11 PM) needs to lock the glass room of the rehearsal room
3. **Return Equipment**: Before leaving, all equipment, public guitars, and various cables outside the rehearsal room door must be moved inside and arranged neatly

## Important Notes

### String Replacement Rule

If you need to replace instrument strings, do so outside the rehearsal room. **String replacement inside the room is prohibited**.

### Public Item Borrowing

If you need to borrow public items from the rehearsal room such as microphone stands, guitar stands, and high stools, **you must communicate with Guitar Association members in advance and obtain permission before borrowing**.
